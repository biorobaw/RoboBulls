****************************************Digital Inputs****************************************************

1 	White 		PWM Set Value 		10% = 0 RPM 90% = 5000 RPM
2 	Brown 		Enable 			Active High
3 	Green 		Direction		Active High or Active Low depending on desired direction
4 	Yellow 		Stop 			Active High
5	Gray		Signal Ground		Connect to the Arduino's ground, extend using a breadboard
6	Pink		+5 VDC			Unused

**********************************************************************************************************
