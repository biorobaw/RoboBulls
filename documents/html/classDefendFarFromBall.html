Detailed Description

The “goalie” behavior of the game.

Author
Narges Ghaedi, James W The robot defends the goal by positioning itself at the goal and moving in between the goal and the ball facing the ball, wherever the ball may be. When the ball becomes close, this behavior moves the robots out and kicks the ball to the center of the field to remove it from the goalie box.
