7 /* DEFEND BEHAVIOR
8  * DefendBehavior is the next iteration of a defense mode (circa May 2015).
9  * It sets robots to sit at points around the goal, and get in the line of motion
10  * of the ball and kick it back if it is coming torwards the goal. In addition,
11  * if the ball is stopped on our side a robot will break to kick it, and also
12  * the robots sway formation to face the ball if it on our side.
13  *
14  * This also serves as a first iteration of an "intelligent agents" type of behavior,
15  * where each robot independently runs the same behavior to without the need for a
16  * strategy. This is made up of `DefendStates` which link to one another.
17  */
