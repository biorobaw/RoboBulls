Detailed Description

Velocity calculation base for differential-type robots.

Author
Muhaimen Shamsi

Closed-Loop control in our case is defined by three constants: krho, kalpha, and kbeta. Each of these have a specific effect on the wheelvelocities returned. ClosedLoopBase implements the movement algorithm.
