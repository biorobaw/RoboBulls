Detailed Description

AttackSupport is a complement to Attackmain, used in NormalGameStrategy.

Author
Muhaimen Shamsi, JamesW

It goes to the most optimal half of the enemy field–the one with least enemies, and the one that the AttackMain robot is not inside of–and waits for a pass from the AttackMain robot.
